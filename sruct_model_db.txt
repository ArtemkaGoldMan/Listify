Model of project:


Tab:
	1. User: userID, telegramUserId, List_of_content, List_of_tags (user one to one list, user one to one list_of_tags)
	2. List_of_content: contentID (List one to many content)
	3. Content: Name, Description, tags (Content one to one {Name, Description}, Content on to many tags)
	4. List_of_tags: tagID (List_of_tags one to many tagID)
	5. Tag: tagID, Name, Description (Tag one to one {Name, Description})
